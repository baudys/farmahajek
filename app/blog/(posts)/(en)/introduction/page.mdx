# {metadata.title}

![image](/blog/uvod/1.webp)

We are glad that you have visited the website of our farm, which for years has been bringing quality products from the love of nature and honest farming. We started this blog to bring you monthly news from our farm, future plans and interesting facts from the world of local farming. Whether you're a regular or just discovering us, here you'll find all the important information regarding not only our products, but also events and news.

## What is happening and what are we planning?

In May, we had a busy time at the farm - for example, we held a Strongman event, which attracted many sports enthusiasts. It was a special opportunity for us to welcome new faces and share our work and products. It's great to see how our farm is alive not only because of the chickens and potatoes, but also because of the community events.

<br />

If you haven't visited our farm yet, don't miss out on our new feature -
the self-service shop, which is open **24/7**. Whether you're in the mood for fresh
eggs or delicious potatoes any time of day, just stop by. Unfortunately.
an unfortunate thing happened to us recently - someone robbed our cash register. This incident made us very
but we're not discouraged. On the contrary, we have tightened security measures and
we thank all our customers for their support.

## Get in touch with us!

We want our blog to be the place where you can find out about everything important that happens on our farm. That's why we'll be providing regular monthly summaries, planning events and explaining the different concepts we're trying out on our farm. If you have any questions or tips for improvement, don't hesitate to contact us - we're here for you.

<br />
We look forward to many more months together full of farm work and the joy we
can to share with you!
